Key Insights
Delivery up vs May. 90.2% delivered across the week vs 88.3% in May, with failure down to 6.3% from 10.5%. List cleaning before each send is paying off.
Native Title is the EOFY hero. The 17 Jun targeted send drove 9 registrations and $4,108 — $1.71 per delivered SMS, far above any other campaign.
Targeted beats broadcast on yield. Native Title, Family Law and Construction all returned $0.20–$1.71 per recipient; the mass state blast returned just $0.15 despite 24.7k delivered.
15 Jun carried the volume. The state blast (9 sends, ~25.8k delivered) was 47% of the week but also the lowest delivery band at ~89% and the bulk of the 381 same-day opt-outs.
Link tracking is live. 354 clicks (0.64% CTR) — new this round via rwt.to/nxt.to links. Succession (33 clicks) and NSW (51) led; useful baseline to optimise against next time.
Watch the small states. General NT delivered only 86.3% — the weakest of the week. Worth checking those numbers were cleaned before the next broadcast.
